About Us

Located at the School of materials Science & Engineering at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore, Our work encompasses Engineering, Physics, Chemistry, Materials Science and Nanotechnology, exploiting novel materials for printed electronics. We are also interested in computation-enabled smart devices, and the use of machine learning surrogate models in creating closed-loop experiments. We are always looking for PhD candidates with *excellent academic results* and research backgrounds in 1) organic/electrochemical transducers 2) low-dimensional material-based devices for IR detection and imaging 3) computational approaches to nanomaterial-based sensors and 4) 3D printing of complex structures. International postdoctoral researchers and fellows for long term (12 months or more) collaborative research visits are very welcome. We strongly encourage student and visitor applications from under-represented groups, regardless of disability, race, religion or belief, sex or sexual orientation. If you are interested, please get in touch with Leonard directly to learn more about our future plans in the above research areas.
